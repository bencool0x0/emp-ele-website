Empower and Elevate

We are looking to offer mentorships and internships for high school students that are at-risk or of underrepresented backgrounds. Our mentorship program connects students with experienced career professionals. We ask our mentors to connect students to internship opportunities at their workplace. Internships serve as a jumpstart to students' careers, providing students with experience and networks in the workplace.

Mentors and mentees are paired based on career interests indicated on our sign-up forms. Our mentorship program is free of charge for students.
