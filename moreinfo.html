Program Guidelines

  • Mentors and mentees will meet for 30 minutes in every month of the semester or school year, with each date being scheduled in the previous meeting.
  • If mentors or mentees need to reschedule the meeting, they must contact the other party at least 2 hours before the meeting.
  • All communication should be between the parent/guardian and mentor unless the parent/guardian gives consent for direct communication to the student.
  • Information about mentees will be kept confidential. Mentors should not share any personal information about their mentee unless written consent has been provided.

Setting

  • Mentors and mentees can choose whether they would prefer to meet in person or online, such as on zoom, by filling in their preferences on the form (or no preference).
    • We will provide mentors with a fully upgraded zoom account.
  • Meetings in person will take place in locations where adults are present such as a public library.
  • Mentors and mentees meeting online through zoom must do the following:
    • Find a quiet, appropriate place to minimize distractions and be able to concentrate on the discussion.
    • Students must behave like they are at school.
    • Parents or guardians should be accessible during the meetings and present for in-person meetings.
